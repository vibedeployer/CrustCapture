Everything your recording needs

No subscriptions. No watermarks. Just a fast native app that does the job.

🔍

Auto Zoom on Click

Automatically zooms into wherever you click, stays zoomed during rapid interactions, and smoothly pans between locations. Looks cinematic with zero effort.

🖱

Cursor Highlights

Configurable cursor glow with click pulse animation. Your viewers always know exactly where to look. Customize color, size, and opacity.

📺

CRT Effect

Scanlines, barrel distortion, chromatic aberration, and vignette. Give your recordings a retro CRT monitor look when you want that extra vibe.

🎨

15+ Backgrounds

Beautiful gradient presets like Ocean, Sunset, and Purple Haze. Or use the custom picker to match your brand. Rounded corners and shadows included.

✂

Trim & Edit

Timeline with thumbnail strip and drag handles. Trim the start and end, scrub through with live preview. Undo and redo everything.

🚀

Fast Export

MP4 with H.264 or HEVC, or export as GIF. Pick your resolution (1080p, 1440p, original) and quality. GPU-accelerated with Metal.
